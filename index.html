🐻 Bear essentials 🐻

  • Joiners only contribute the 1st expedition skill of the leftmost hero.
  • Rally leaders provide almost everything else: stats, gear, widgets, buffs, march size, etc.
  • Bear damage heavily favors Lethality/Attack over defensive stats.
  • Chance-based skills average out over the 10 attack rounds, so consistency matters.

🐻 Bear Rally Effects 🐻

🔰 Rally Leader Provides 🔰

  • Hero stats (all 3 heroes)
  • Rally capacity
  • Gear & tech
  • Widgets & island buffs

🔺 Rally Joiner Provides 🔺

  • Troop count
  • 1st skill of leftmost hero

🐻 Bear Basics 🐻

Bear is all about offensive stats:

  1. Lethality
  2. Attack

Defensive stats matter very little.

Bear attacks happen over 10 rounds, so chance-based skills average out over time.

Example: A skill with a 50% chance to increase damage by 50% gives roughly:

  • ~25% average extra damage over the full attack.

🔰 Rally Leader Setup 🔰

Leaders should use:

  • Best offensive heroes
  • Best gear
  • Best widgets/buffs

🔰 Recommended leader heroes 🔰

Infantry

  • Amadeus / Zoe

Cavalry

  • Petra

Archer

  • Rosa / Yang

If you don’t have these at strong star levels, use your best offensive replacements.

For troop optimization, use Frankinator’s Bear Ratio Calculator.

🔺 Rally Joiner Setup 🔺

Only 2 things matter for joiners:

1️⃣ Leftmost Hero

Only the first expedition skill of the leftmost hero affects the rally.

2️⃣ Troop Ratio

Recommended troop ratio:

  • 1 / 10 / 89
  • Or as close as possible.

If your troop count is still low, focus more on sending full marches rather than perfect ratios.

2nd and 3rd heroes can be anything
